Starlight Delight

Description

Embark on a journey of celestial flavors with our Starlight Delight. This enchanting coffee
creation combines the richness of velvety espresso with a hint of ethereal sweetness,
leaving your taste buds dancing among the stars.

Ingridients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 oz vanilla syrup
  • 1/2 cup steamed milk
  • Whipped cream for topping
  • Edible glitter (optional)

Instructions

  1. a shot of espresso and pour it into your favorite mug.
  2. Add the vanilla syrup and stir gently.
  3. Steam the milk and pour it over the espresso mixture.
  4. Top it off with a dollop of whipped cream.
  5. Sprinkle edible glitter for that extra touch of magic.
  6. Sip and let the celestial journey begin!
