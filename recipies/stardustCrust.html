Stardust Crust

Description

Brace yourself for a taste sensation that transcends the ordinary - Stardust Crust.
This cosmic concoction pairs the robustness of coffee with a touch of celestial sweetness,
creating an otherworldly experience in every sip.

Ingridients

  • 1 cup cold brew coffee
  • 2 oz caramel syrup
  • 1/2 cup milk (your choice)
  • Ice cubes
  • Caramel drizzle for garnish

Instructions

  1. a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour cold brew coffee over the ice.
  3. Add caramel syrup and stir well.
  4. Pour in your choice of milk.
  5. Drizzle caramel on top for that stardust touch.
  6. Stir and enjoy the cosmic infusion of flavors.
