Cosmic Coca Moca

Description

Indulge in the cosmic fusion of cocoa and coffee with our Cosmic Coca Moca.
This delightful blend of rich chocolate and espresso will
take your taste buds to a galaxy of decadence.

Ingridients

  • 1.5 oz chocolate syrup
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/2 cup steamed milk
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for topping

Instructions

  1. Prepare a shot of espresso and pour it into a mug.
  2. Add the chocolate syrup and stir until well combined.
  3. Steam the milk and pour it over the chocolate-espresso mixture.
  4. Top it off with a generous layer of whipped cream.
  5. Sprinkle chocolate shavings for a cosmic finish.
  6. Savor the velvety blend of cocoa and coffee in every sip.
